## Getting Started with Audiobookshelf

Audiobookshelf is a self-hosted audiobook server. You can listen to audiobooks directly in your browser or via the Audiobookshelf app.

### Creating Your Account

You'll receive an invite link from me with login credentials. Use those to sign in the first time.

### Listening to Audiobooks

- **Browser**: Open Audiobookshelf using the link above and click on any book to start listening.
- **Mobile app**: Download **Audiobookshelf** from the App Store or Google Play, then enter the server address and your credentials.

The app remembers your progress across devices — pick up where you left off on any device.

### Features

- **Progress sync** across browser and app.
- **Sleep timer** — great for listening in bed.
- **Playback speed** control.
- **Bookmarks** to save your favorite moments.

## Requesting Audiobooks with ReadMeAbook

Don't see a book you want? Use ReadMeAbook to request it.

### How to Request

1. Open ReadMeAbook using the link above.
2. Search for the audiobook title or author.
3. Submit the request.

I'll review requests and add available titles as soon as I can. If a book isn't easily available, I'll let you know.

### Notes

- Requests for popular titles are usually fulfilled quickly.
- Some older or niche titles may take longer or may not be available.
- Feel free to request multiple books at once.
