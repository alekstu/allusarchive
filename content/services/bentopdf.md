## Getting Started with BentoPDF

BentoPDF is a self-hosted PDF toolkit. It lets you work with PDF files directly in your browser — no uploads to third-party services.

### What You Can Do

- **Merge** multiple PDFs into one.
- **Split** a PDF into separate pages or page ranges.
- **Compress** a PDF to reduce file size.
- **Convert** images to PDF or PDF to images.
- **Rotate** pages.
- **Remove pages** from a PDF.
- **Add passwords** to protect a PDF.

### How to Use

1. Open BentoPDF using the link above.
2. Choose the operation you want from the main menu.
3. Upload your file(s).
4. Download the result.

All processing happens on the server — your files are not sent to any third party.

### Tips

- For best compression results, use the **Compress PDF** tool and choose the quality level that fits your needs.
- When merging, you can drag and drop files to reorder them before merging.
- Passwords added via BentoPDF use standard AES-256 encryption.
